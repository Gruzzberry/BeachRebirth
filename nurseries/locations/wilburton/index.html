Introduction

Opened in 2015 Beach Babies in Wilburton has 3 separate barn conversion buildings situated in an acre and a half of land. One building provides the 3 base rooms for each age group. The next houses a big room we use for cooking and eating with the older age groups and the third is being is dedicated to the preschoolers. Beach Babies in Wilburton is rated Outstanding by Ofsted.

Learning & Development

What can your child expect?

We want to meet your child’s needs at their own pace as they become more at ease with self-driven learning and exploration through play. We nurture children to enable them to become confident, resilient and joyful learners.

The Beach Babies way

At Beach Babies we provide a nature-led environment and outstanding childcare professionals to allow your children to grow and thrive. We care for and educate children from 10 months to 5 years old, tailoring what we offer to your child’s interests and need. Raising a child is a partnership with many different elements, we work with you to support your child and your family.

We are helping to grow a community and many of our families stay in touch for years, some of our first children and their parents have returned to become members of our team.
