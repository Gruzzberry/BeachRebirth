Careers

Do you want to really make a difference to people lives? Working in a wonderful early years setting can be the way to do that. Your guidance and support are key to helping children develop not only physical and knowledge skills but also the attitudes that will stay with them for life.

With your help they become curious, independent and resilient learners. Their parents will be guided by your advice and professional knowledge and you and your team will not only provide superb care and education but could really change a family’s life.

We remain part of our children’s lives for many years, welcoming them back for reunions and recently as new members of our working team. It really does feel like you are part of an extended family.

Level 3, Early Years EducatorJanuary, 2025
Job Responsibilities

We are looking for full-time Early Years Educators / Nursery Practitioners to join our friendly, enthusiastic and hard-working family at the Landbeach Nursery, just outside Cambridge.

What you’ll do:
  • Greet children each day with a warm, kind smile that makes them feel loved and welcomed.
  • Be always ready to build a fully functional space rocket from a kitchen roll tube, 2 elastic bands, and a handful of pipe cleaners.
  • Share songs, stories, and creative activities that everyone will enjoy (being in tune is optional, but knowing Frozen lyrics is a plus!).
  • Navigate a classroom that sometimes looks like a hurricane just hit—and do it with calm and grace.
  • Multitask like a pro, reading stories, answering questions, and explaining how the world works (sometimes all at once).
  • Encourage independence, even if it means waiting patiently while a child learns to zip up their jacket.
  • Gently mediate the children’s disagreements over who has more pesto pasta and celebrate milestones, big or small—from mastering the alphabet to learning to share.
  • Ideally be able to correctly and immediately name and draw all species of dinosaur from Aardonyx to Zuniceratops.
  • Flex those leg muscles—getting up and down from tiny chairs while keeping our space safe and engaging.
What we’re looking for:
  • Ideally a Level 3 Childcare qualification (or equivalent). But your passion is more important to us.
  • Enthusiasm for working with young children and creating a nurturing, supportive environment.
  • Excellent communication skills to engage with children, parents, and colleagues.
  • Patience, creativity, and a good sense of humour (you’ll need it for all those “why” questions!).
In return, we’ll offer:
  • A fun, exciting, and dynamic environment—no two days are the same!
  • A supportive, collaborative team that feels like family.
  • The chance to build close, long-lasting relationships with the children and their families.
  • Appreciation just for being you.
  • Signing on bonus!
  • 40-hour working week (working over 4 or 5 days)
  • Competitive salary, paying the REAL LIVING WAGE.
  • Starting with the equivalent of 4 weeks paid holidays, rising to 5 weeks over 2 years. Plus the week between Christmas and New Year, Christmas Eve if it is a working week, bank holidays and a half day holiday on your birthday (or closest working day to it).
  • Discounted childcare, currently at £51 per day. We also take the new nursery funding.
  • Half-day holiday on your birthday!

If you’re up for becoming a much loved special person who the children and their families will remember for decades to come – please get in touch.

To apply please submit your application to ali@beachbabies.co.uk
